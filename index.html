GAZPACHO
The aim of Gazpacho is to guess the 6-letter word from the clue. First, play words of 3, 4, then 5 letters to eliminate letters — anything you play disappears from the keyboard. But any letter that belongs to the 6-letter word is forbidden: use one and you lose a life. Lose 4 lives and the attempt is over. Make it to the final round and guess wrong, and you restart — but you only get 2 attempts each day. You can't reuse any word you've played in the last 7 days.
Going for gold: cracked the clue straight away? Don't coast — dodge every forbidden letter and solve it on your first attempt to earn the Golden Gazpacho ⭐
Play every day to keep the streak alive!
